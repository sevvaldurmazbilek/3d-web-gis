Welcome to Your Interactive 3D Apartment Explorer!

Find your perfect sunny and accessible apartment in Munich and Helsinki with ease.

How to Use:

Explore Cities in 3D: Drag your mouse to navigate through the city. Use the zoom feature for a closer look.

Sunlight Simulation: Adjust the date and time sliders to ensure your new home has just the right amount of sunlight.

Assess Travel Convenience: Click on the accessibility map's hexagons to view average travel times for each area and make an informed decision about your location.

Helsinki at a Click: Interested in Helsinki? Just flip the switch and transport yourself to a detailed map of Helsinki.
